# 🌍 About YatraPass

YatraPass is a modern digital public transportation platform built to replace traditional cash-based fare collection with a secure, intelligent, and fully digital experience.

Passengers simply scan a QR code when boarding a bus, begin their journey, and receive automatic fare calculation based on the official route and distance travelled. Payments are securely deducted from the passenger's wallet, while digital receipts and trip history are generated instantly.

Rather than being just another payment application, YatraPass is designed as a **complete public transportation ecosystem** connecting passengers, drivers, transport operators, and administrators through a single platform.

# 🎯 Vision

> **To become Nepal's national digital public transportation platform by making every journey smarter, safer, and completely cashless.**

# 🚀 Why YatraPass?

Public transportation in Nepal still depends heavily on cash transactions, creating unnecessary friction for passengers, conductors, and transport operators.

YatraPass solves these challenges by introducing:

* 🚍 Cashless Public Transportation
* 📱 QR-Based Boarding
* 💳 Digital Wallet
* 📍 Smart Fare Calculation
* 📄 Automatic Digital Receipts
* 📊 Centralized Transport Management
* 📈 Real-Time Analytics
